Available Datasets

🏗️

Facility Master

Master list of healthcare facilities containing facility IDs, their block locations, and facility types used as a reference for linking other datasets

👥

Manav Sampada

Healthcare workforce dataset capturing employee postings, designations, facility assignments, and employment status

🏥

Equipment Registry

Registry of medical equipment installed at healthcare facilities including equipment type, installation date, functional status, and maintenance records

📊

HMIS Indicators

Monthly HMIS service delivery dataset recording OPD visits, institutional deliveries, C-sections, and major surgeries for each facility
